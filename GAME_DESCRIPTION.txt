================================================================================
                    COUNTRY LIFE - Game Description
================================================================================

GENRE:        Open-World Farming & Life Simulation
SETTING:      Rural Vietnamese countryside
ENGINE:       Unity (URP)
PERSPECTIVE:  First-person

================================================================================
                          OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Country Life is a first-person open-world farming and life simulation game set
in a rural Vietnamese countryside. The player arrives by car at a small village
and must build a life from scratch: farm crops, build structures, trade with
merchants, defeat monsters, and complete quests.

The game features a full day/night cycle, farming mechanics, a construction
system, NPC shops, enemy encounters, a pet companion, and a story with two
possible endings (happy and sad).

================================================================================
                          CONTROLS
================================================================================

Movement:
  W/A/S/D          Move
  Space             Jump
  Left Shift        Sprint (costs stamina)
  Mouse             Look around

Tools & Items:
  Left Click        Use selected tool/item
  Right Click       Open shop (near NPC/Buffalo)
  E                 Interact / Pick up nearby items
  Q                 Drop selected item
  R                 Reload gun / Rotate building preview (Hammer)
  F                 Open building selection menu (Hammer only)
  Escape            Deselect hammer / Close menus

Inventory:
  1-0               Select inventory slot 1-10
  [ / ]             Cycle inventory slots

Building:
  T                 Rotate building preview

UI:
  Escape            Pause menu
  F5                Play intro cutscene (debug)
  F6                Happy ending (debug)
  F7                Sad ending (debug)

                          TOOLS & ITEMS
================================================================================

TOOLS (with stamina cost per use):

  Axe               15 stamina  - Chop trees, break branches, split wood debris
  Pickaxe           15 stamina  - Mine rocks, smash rock debris
  Hoe               12 stamina  - Till ground into farmable field tiles
  Hammer            20 stamina  - Place building blueprints, damage/remove

  Scythe            10 stamina  - Harvest mature crops (stage 4), cut grass
  Watering Can       8 stamina  - Water crops to help them grow
  Fertilizer         5 stamina  - Fertilize crops to boost growth (consumes 1)
  Gun                5 stamina  - Shoot to destroy trees and rocks at range

GUN SYSTEM:
  Max Ammo:         6 bullets
  Shoot Range:      20 units
  Ammo Item:        "ammo" (found as pickup)
  Reload:           Press R, consumes ammo items from inventory
  Effect:           Destroys trees and rocks on hit

================================================================================
                          FARMING
================================================================================

FARMING PROCESS:
  1. Use HOE on ground to create a tilled field tile
  2. Select a SEED in inventory
  3. Left-click on the field to plant
  4. Water with WATERING CAN to help growth
  5. Fertilize with FERTILIZER for faster growth (consumes 1 fertilizer)
  6. Wait for crops to reach Stage 4 (fully grown)
  7. Harvest with SCYTHE

GROWTH:
  - 4 growth stages total (Stage 0 = empty, Stage 4 = harvestable)
  - Each stage takes 4 seconds to grow
  - Total growth time: ~16 seconds
  - Watering and fertilizing speed up growth

================================================================================
                          BUILDING SYSTEM
================================================================================

HAMMER TOOL:
  - Left-click in demolish mode: destroy buildings, remove blueprints
  - Press F: open building selection menu
  - Choose a structure from the menu
  - Left-click in place mode: place blueprint (even through existing structures)
  - After placing, automatically returns to demolish mode
  - R key: rotate building preview
  - Escape: deselect hammer

BUILDING PROCESS:
  1. Equip HAMMER
  2. Press F to open building menu
  3. Select a building type
  4. Place the blueprint (transparent ghost shows where)
  5. Carry WOOD or STONE resources near the blueprint
  6. Resources auto-deposit when nearby
  7. Building completes when fully supplied

================================================================================
                          QUESTS
================================================================================

Three quests available:

  1. HARVEST WHEAT
     Target: collect 100 wheat
     Reward: 250 gold

  2. SLAY MONSTERS
     Target: defeat 30 enemies
     Reward: 500 gold

  3. EARN COINS
     Target: accumulate 100,000 gold
     Reward: 1,000 gold

All quests completed triggers the HAPPY ENDING cutscene.

================================================================================
                          SAVE SYSTEM
================================================================================

  Format:           JSON
  Location:         Application.persistentDataPath/savegame.json
  Saved Data:
    - Current day and time of day
    - Player position, rotation, HP, stamina, money
    - Full inventory (all 10 slots with item type and count)
    - Gun ammo count
    - All crop fields (position, crop type, growth stage, watered, fertilized)
    - All placed buildings (type, position, rotation)
    - Quest progress and completion status

================================================================================
                          STORY & ENDINGS
================================================================================

INTRO CUTSCENE:
  A blue car drives along the road into the village. The player arrives
  to start their new life in the countryside.

HAPPY ENDING (all quests completed):
  Player walks with Teto (pink-haired companion NPC) down a path.
  Hearts appear. End screen: "HAPPY ENDING"
  "Ban va Teto da cung nhau den cuoi con duong!"
  (You and Teto have walked together to the end of the road!)
  Player can press Enter to continue playing.

SAD ENDING (triggered manually / fail state):
  A wagon with a groom and wife NPC travels down the road.
  End screen: "KET THUC DAU BUONG" (Sad Ending)

================================================================================
                          TECHNICAL NOTES
================================================================================

  - All UI is code-generated (no prefabs for HUD or menus)
  - Unity New Input System (Keyboard.current, Mouse.current)
  - CharacterController for player movement
  - URP (Universal Render Pipeline) for rendering
  - Building preview: translucent ghost cube follows crosshair
  - Building menu: ScrollRect with VerticalLayoutGroup for scrollable list
  - Inventory HUD: always visible at bottom of screen
  - Stats HUD: always visible at top-left of screen
